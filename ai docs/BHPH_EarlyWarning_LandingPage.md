## WHAT IS IT?

### The BHPH Early Warning Agent — here's exactly what you're getting.

Early Warning is an AI-powered agent purpose-built for Buy Here, Pay Here dealers. It runs in the background of your portfolio — not your front-end sales process, not your DMS management — just your active accounts and collections performance.

**Here's how it works:**

**Step 1 — Connect your DMS.** Early Warning integrates with iDMS, DealerCenter, Wayne Reaves, DealerPack, AutoManager, and others via API or simple file import. Setup takes less than a day. No IT department required.

**Step 2 — The agent contacts your customers.** On a configurable cadence — weekly or bi-weekly — the system sends a friendly, brief, branded text message to each active account. These are not collection messages. They are lightweight check-ins, phrased naturally, that give the customer an easy opening to share what's going on. Payment reminders are also included 2–3 days before each due date to reduce friction.

**Step 3 — The AI listens for risk signals.** Every response is analyzed for indicators of account risk: car trouble, job loss, financial hardship, ghosting risk, relocation, or dissatisfaction. The system is trained specifically on BHPH account failure patterns — not generic customer service language.

**Step 4 — Your team gets a daily alert digest.** Each morning, your collector or manager sees a prioritized list of accounts that triggered a warning signal overnight, with the context of what the customer said and a recommended action. No wading through a full portfolio list. Just the accounts that need you today.

**Step 5 — Your team makes the call.** Early Warning does not negotiate, does not make demands, and does not attempt to resolve the account on its own. It surfaces the signal and hands it to your human team while the relationship is still intact. That's the White Hat Way — technology that makes human relationships stronger, not a replacement for them.

**Compliance built in:** All messaging is TCPA-compliant. Customer opt-in is captured at deal signing using a simple addendum provided during onboarding.

**Dashboard included:** Monthly reporting shows accounts flagged, actions taken, interventions made, and estimated accounts preserved — so you can see exactly what the tool is doing for your portfolio.
